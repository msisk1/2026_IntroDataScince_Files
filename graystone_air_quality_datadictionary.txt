DATASET: Graystone City Neighborhood Air Quality and Health Outcomes
SOURCE: Fictional dataset created for Introduction to Data Science using Python (Chapter 3)
CITY: Graystone (fictional mid-sized U.S. city, ~280,000 residents)
UNIT OF ANALYSIS: Neighborhood (N=50)
TIME PERIOD: 2020-2024
CREATED BY: Course dataset, not real data. Patterns are illustrative.

KNOWN LIMITATIONS:
- All data are simulated. Neighborhood names, boundaries, and values are fictional.
- PM2.5 and asthma_er_rate values are correlated by design to illustrate equity patterns.
- station_last_calibrated is intentionally missing for some neighborhoods to illustrate
  metadata gaps in real-world environmental monitoring data.
- Data sources are mixed (City EPA Monitor, Community Sensor, Estimated), reflecting
  real-world variation in data quality and collection method.
- Population values are approximate and not derived from actual Census data.

VARIABLES:

neighborhood_id
  Type: string
  Description: Unique identifier for each neighborhood. Format: [district code]-[number].
  Valid values: NW (North), NE (East), SW (West), SE (South), DT (Downtown)
  Notes: Treat as a categorical identifier, not a number.

neighborhood_name
  Type: string
  Description: Common name for the neighborhood.

district
  Type: string (categorical)
  Description: City district the neighborhood belongs to.
  Valid values: North, East, West, South, Downtown

pop_2020
  Type: integer
  Description: Estimated residential population as of 2020.
  Units: Number of residents
  Notes: Approximate. Not derived from actual Census data.

pct_nonwhite
  Type: float
  Description: Proportion of residents who identify as non-white.
  Units: Proportion (0.0 to 1.0). Multiply by 100 for percentage.
  Notes: Derived from simulated demographic data. Does not distinguish between
         specific racial or ethnic groups.

median_household_income
  Type: integer
  Description: Median annual household income for neighborhood residents.
  Units: U.S. dollars (nominal, 2020)

pm25_annual_mean
  Type: float
  Description: Annual mean concentration of fine particulate matter (PM2.5).
  Units: Micrograms per cubic meter (ug/m3)
  Notes: EPA 24-hour standard is 35 ug/m3. EPA annual standard is 12 ug/m3.
         Values above 12 ug/m3 exceed the annual standard.
         For neighborhoods without a City EPA Monitor, values are estimated
         from community sensors or interpolated from adjacent stations.
         Estimated values carry higher uncertainty.

pm25_days_above_standard
  Type: integer
  Description: Number of days per year on which the daily PM2.5 reading
               exceeded the EPA 24-hour standard of 35 ug/m3.
  Units: Days per year

tree_canopy_pct
  Type: float
  Description: Proportion of neighborhood land area covered by tree canopy.
  Units: Proportion (0.0 to 1.0). Multiply by 100 for percentage.
  Notes: Derived from simulated land cover data.

asthma_er_rate
  Type: float
  Description: Rate of emergency room visits with a primary diagnosis of asthma.
  Units: ER visits per 1,000 residents per year
  Notes: Reflects reported ER visits only. Does not capture residents who
         sought care at urgent care clinics, primary care providers, or who
         did not seek care at all.

monitoring_station
  Type: boolean (True/False)
  Description: Whether a City EPA air quality monitoring station is located
               within or immediately adjacent to the neighborhood.
  Notes: Neighborhoods without a station (False) have pm25_annual_mean values
         derived from community sensors or estimation, not direct measurement.

station_last_calibrated
  Type: date (YYYY-MM-DD) or blank
  Description: Date of most recent calibration for the neighborhood's City EPA
               monitoring station.
  Notes: Blank if monitoring_station is False (no station present).
         Also blank for some stations where calibration records were not
         systematically maintained. Missing calibration dates indicate
         uncertainty about measurement accuracy for those stations.
         This is an intentional data quality issue for instructional purposes.

data_source
  Type: string (categorical)
  Description: Primary source of the pm25_annual_mean value for this neighborhood.
  Valid values:
    City EPA Monitor   -- Direct measurement from a calibrated City EPA station
    Community Sensor   -- Low-cost sensor operated by a community organization;
                          higher measurement uncertainty than EPA monitors
    Estimated          -- Interpolated from adjacent stations or modeled;
                          highest uncertainty, should be interpreted with caution
