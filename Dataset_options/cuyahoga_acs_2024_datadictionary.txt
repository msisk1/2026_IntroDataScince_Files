DATASET: Cuyahoga County Census Tract Demographic and Economic Data
SOURCE: U.S. Census Bureau, American Community Survey (ACS) 5-Year Estimates, 2020-2024
GEOGRAPHY: Census tracts within Cuyahoga County, Ohio
UNIT OF ANALYSIS: Census tract (N=approximately 450)
TIME PERIOD: 2020-2024 (5-year pooled estimates)
ACCESSED: June 15, 2026
FILE: cuyahoga_acs_2024.csv

ABOUT THIS DATASET:
The American Community Survey is an ongoing survey conducted by the U.S. Census Bureau
that collects detailed demographic, social, economic, and housing information about the
U.S. population. The 5-year estimates pool five years of survey responses to produce
reliable estimates for small geographies like census tracts. Each row in this dataset
represents one census tract in Cuyahoga County, Ohio.

KNOWN LIMITATIONS:
- ACS estimates are based on a sample, not a complete count. All values carry a margin
  of error that is not included in this simplified dataset. Users making policy or
  research decisions should consult the full ACS data including margins of error at
  data.census.gov.
- 5-year estimates represent an average over the 2020-2024 period, not a snapshot of
  any single year. Rapidly changing neighborhoods may not be accurately captured.
- Some census tracts have very small populations, which increases the uncertainty of
  their estimates. Tracts with populations under 500 should be interpreted with
  particular caution.
- Census tract boundaries occasionally change between decennial censuses. The boundaries
  used here are from the 2020 Census.
- The nonwhite_count variable is calculated as total_race minus white_alone and therefore
  includes all persons who did not identify as white alone, including those who identified
  as multiracial.

VARIABLE REFERENCE:

geoid
  Type: string
  Description: Unique identifier for each census tract, combining state, county, and
               tract FIPS codes.
  Example: "39035010100" (39=Ohio, 035=Cuyahoga County, 010100=tract number)
  ACS Table: Derived from geography
  Notes: Always treat as a string, not a number. Leading zeros are significant.

tract_name
  Type: string
  Description: Human-readable census tract name including county and state.
  Example: "Census Tract 101, Cuyahoga County, Ohio"
  ACS Table: Derived from geography

total_pop
  Type: integer
  Description: Estimated total population of the census tract.
  Units: Number of persons
  ACS Table: B01003_001

median_hh_income
  Type: integer
  Description: Estimated median annual household income.
  Units: U.S. dollars (2024 inflation-adjusted)
  ACS Table: B19013_001
  Notes: May be NA for tracts with very small populations or insufficient survey
         responses.

poverty_count
  Type: integer
  Description: Estimated number of persons below the federal poverty level for whom
               poverty status was determined.
  Units: Number of persons
  ACS Table: B17001_002
  Notes: Does not include persons for whom poverty status was not determined
         (e.g., persons in institutional group quarters).

bachelors_up_count
  Type: integer
  Description: Estimated number of persons aged 25 and older with a bachelor's degree
               or higher (sum of bachelor's, master's, professional, and doctoral
               degrees).
  Units: Number of persons
  ACS Tables: B15003_022 + B15003_023 + B15003_024 + B15003_025
  Notes: Derived variable; sum of four ACS estimates.

owner_occupied
  Type: integer
  Description: Estimated number of owner-occupied housing units.
  Units: Number of housing units
  ACS Table: B25003_002

nonwhite_count
  Type: integer
  Description: Estimated number of persons who did not identify as white alone.
               Calculated as total_race minus white_alone.
  Units: Number of persons
  ACS Tables: B02001_001 minus B02001_002
  Notes: Includes persons identifying as multiracial. This is a derived variable,
         not a direct ACS estimate.

median_gross_rent
  Type: integer
  Description: Estimated median monthly gross rent (including utilities) for
               renter-occupied housing units.
  Units: U.S. dollars per month (2024 inflation-adjusted)
  ACS Table: B25064_001
  Notes: May be NA for tracts with very few rental units or insufficient survey
         responses.
