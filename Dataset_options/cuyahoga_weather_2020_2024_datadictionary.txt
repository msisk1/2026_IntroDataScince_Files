NWS Airport stations are operated by the National Weather Service and record
temperature, precipitation, snowfall, and wind speed. These are professional-grade
instruments with calibration and maintenance requirements.

CoCoRaHS (Community Collaborative Rain, Hail and Snow Network) stations are operated
by trained volunteers. They record precipitation and snowfall only. Temperature and
wind speed are not measured at these stations and will appear as NA in the dataset.
This is expected, not a data error.

================================================================
IMPORTANT NOTES ON DATA INTERPRETATION
================================================================

MISSING VALUES
  NA in temp_max_f, temp_min_f, wind_speed_mph for CoCoRaHS stations:
    Expected -- these instruments do not measure temperature or wind.

  NA in precip_in or snowfall_in for any station:
    Indicates no observation was submitted for that day, either due to
    equipment issues or (for CoCoRaHS) volunteer observer absence.

  A value of 0 in precip_in or snowfall_in:
    Indicates a valid observation of no precipitation. This is different
    from NA.

UNITS
  Data were downloaded from NOAA Climate Data Online in standard U.S. units:
    Temperature: degrees Fahrenheit
    Precipitation: inches
    Snowfall: inches
    Wind speed: miles per hour

LAKE EFFECT
  Burke Lakefront Airport (USW00004853) is located approximately 200 meters
  from the shore of Lake Erie. Lake Erie moderates temperatures at this location
  (warmer winters, cooler summers than inland) and produces substantially higher
  snowfall during lake-effect snow events compared to inland stations. Users
  comparing the two airport stations should account for this geographic difference.

BURKE LAKEFRONT AIRPORT
  Cleveland Burke Lakefront Airport closed to commercial air traffic in 2023.
  The NOAA weather station at this location remains active and continues to
  report observations.

VARIABLES
================================================================

station_id ......... NOAA GHCN station identifier (string)
station_name ....... Official NOAA station name
station_type ....... Airport (NWS) or Volunteer (CoCoRaHS)
latitude ........... Decimal degrees, WGS84
longitude .......... Decimal degrees, WGS84
date ............... Observation date (YYYY-MM-DD; stored as string -- convert
                     with pd.to_datetime() for time-based analysis)
temp_max_f ......... Maximum daily temperature (degrees Fahrenheit; NA for CoCoRaHS)
temp_min_f ......... Minimum daily temperature (degrees Fahrenheit; NA for CoCoRaHS)
precip_in .......... Total daily liquid precipitation (inches; 0 = dry day, NA = no obs)
snowfall_in ........ Total daily snowfall (inches; 0 = no snow, NA = no obs)
wind_speed_mph ..... Mean daily wind speed (miles per hour; NA for CoCoRaHS)
